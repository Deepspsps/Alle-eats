Cheeseballs

45 Mins
X

Ingredients

500 grams potatoes
1 cup bread crumbs
2 tablespoon chopped corriander and onions
2 tablespoon lemon juice
½ tablespoon red chilli powder, or less to taste
1 teaspoon garlic paste
1 teaspoon cumin powder
100 gram cheddar cheese, cut into ½ inch cubes
¼ teaspoon red chilli flakes and dried basil
¼ teaspoon black pepper
2 tablespoon cornflour
1-2 eggs , as needed

Directions

Step 1:


Wash and boil potatoes in a large pot with salt. Peel off the skin and mash potatoes until smooth. Add all ingredients of seasoning to the mashed potato and mix well.

Step 2:


Cut cheese into 25 cubes. Season cheese by mixing red chili, black pepper, and herbs in it.

Step 3:


Roll 25 balls out of mashed potato and simultaneously inserting cheese cubes into each ball. Make sure the cheese cube is completely covered with potato.

Step 4:


Beat eggs with salt, red chilli powder and water. Set aside.

Step 5:


Roll all ball first in cornflour. Then roll each ball in egg wash and then in bread crumbs. For double coating, dip each balls again in egg wash and then in bread crumb.

Step 6:


Place balls in fridge for 30 minute to firm.

Step 7:


Deep fry potato cheese ball in small batches on medium heat for 2-3 minutes then increase heat and fry on high heat for 1-2 minutes until golden and crispy.
