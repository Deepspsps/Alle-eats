Ingredients

2 ¼ cups All purpose flour
1 teaspoon Baking soda
¼ teaspoon Salt (skip if using salted butter)
2 sticks of Unsalted butter (soften to room temperature)
½ cup White granulated sugar
½ cup Light brown sugar
1 teaspoon Pure vanilla extract
¼ cup Milk
1 cup Chocolate chips


Directions

Step 1:


Take all purpose flour, salt and baking soda in a bowl and whisk it until mixed. Keep it aside.

Step 2:


In another bowl, take softened butter. Beat it using a wire whisk until smooth. If using a stand mixer or hand mixer then skip beating only butter. Go with the next step straight away beating butter and sugar together.

Step 3:


Add white sugar and brown sugar. Beat it until smooth and creamy.

Step 4:


Add milk and vanilla. Beat again until mixed. Add dry flour mixture. Again beat until it comes together like a dough. If doing by hand, then use a spatula and not a wire whisk.

Step 5:


Add chocolate chips. Fold the chocolate chips into the dough. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and chill the dough for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

Step 6:


Preheat the oven to 350 F or 180 C for at least 10 minutes. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat and keep it ready.

Step 7:


Make around 1 inch size of balls from the chilled cookie dough. Place on the cookie sheet a few inches apart from each other, because they will spread as bake.

Step 8:


Bake them for 10-12 minutes in preheated oven or until the edges look set and light brown in color and the center still looks soft. Let it cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. After that, transfer them to a cooling rack to cool them completely.

Ingredients

1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white and brown sugar each
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons hot water
½ teaspoon salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Step 1:


Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.

Step 2:


Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 3:


Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.

Step 4:


Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

Step 5:


Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

Step 6:


Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.

Step 7:


Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.

Step 8:


Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
