Punjabi Mushroom Curry

30 Mins
X

Ingredients

1 tsp cumin
1 inch cinnamon, 5 cloves, 3 cardamom pods
2 onions and tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
½ tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp chilli powder and coriander powder
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp curd
200 grams mushroom, chopped
2 chilli
2 tbsp cream
1 tsp kasuri methi, crushed
½ tsp garam masala

Directions

Step 1:


Firstly, in a cooker heat 2 tbsp oil. add 1 tsp cumin, 1 inch cinnamon, 5 cloves, 3 pods cardamom, and saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Step 2:

Add 2 onions, 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste and saute until the onions turn golden brown.

Step 3:

Further, keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, and saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Step 4:

Now add 2 tomato, 1 tsp salt and cook until the tomatoes turn soft and mushy. i have chopped tomatoes in the chopper, to hasten the cooking process.

Step 5:

Once the oil separates from the tomatoes, add 2 tbsp curd and cook well. Furthermore, add 200 grams mushroom, 2 chilli, and mix gently.

Step 6:

Now add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting the consistency as required. Add 1 tbsp butter, cover, and pressure cook for 2 whistles.

Step 7:

Once the pressure settles down, add 2 tbsp cream, 1 tsp kasuri methi, ½ tsp garam masala, and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.

Step 8:

Finally, enjoy mushroom masala curry recipe with roti or rice.
