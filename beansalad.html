Bean Salad

20 Mins
X

Ingredients

1 can kidney beans
1 can black beans
½ cup chopped green pepper
½ cup chopped onion
½ cup chopped celery
½ cup olive oil
½ cup vinegar
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground black pepper



Directions

Step 1:


Gather all ingredients.

Step 2:


Combine kidney beans, black beans, green pepper, onion, and celery in a large bowl; toss to mix.

Step 3:


Whisk together oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl; pour over bean mixture. Mix well.

Step 4:


Refrigerate salad for 8 hours to overnight before serving.
