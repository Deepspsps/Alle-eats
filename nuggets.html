Ingredients

6 large potatoes
1 tbsp tomato paste
1 onion
1 bunch spring onion
¾ cup bread crumbs
½ tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp salt
½ tsp black pepper
½ tsp chili flakes
oil for frying

Directions

Step 1:


Boil the potatoes with skins on for about 45 minutes or until fork tender.

Step 2:


Peel and cool down the potatoes.

Step 3:


With the potato masher, mash the potatoes very well.

Step 4:


Add chopped onion, spring onion, tomato paste, spices and breadcrumbs and mix everything with your hands.

Step 5:


Form little nuggets and fry in the preheated oil until both sides are crispy and brown.

Step 6:


Serve it with your favourite sauce and enjoy!

Ingredients

vegetable oil for frying
4 cups all-purpose flour
6 tablespoons garlic salt
3 tablespoons ground black pepper
4 large eggs, beaten
8 boneless chicken breast halves - cut into small chunks





Directions

Step 1:


Heat 1 inch oil in a large skillet or saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2:


Stir together flour, garlic salt, and pepper in a bowl. Dip chicken pieces into beaten eggs, then press each piece into flour mixture to coat well

Step 3:


shake off excess flour. Place coated chicken pieces onto a plate.

Step 4:


Working in batches, fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown and no longer pink in the center.

Step 5:


An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
