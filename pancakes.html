Pancakes

20 Mins

Ingredients

1 1⁄4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 tablespoon sugar
1⁄4 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons water




Directions

Step 1:


Mix together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in medium bowl.

Step 2:


Combine milk, oil and water. Add to dry ingredients.

Step 3:


Stir just until moistened.

Step 4:


Lightly spray a pan with non-stick cooking spray or lightly wipe with oil. Heat the pan over medium-high heat

Step 5:


For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot pan.

Step 6:


Pancakes are ready to turn when tops are bubbly all over, a few bubbles have burst, and the edges begin to appear dry.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/3 cup whole milk
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons melted unsalted butter



Directions

Step 1:


In a medium bowl, whisk flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a large bowl, whisk milk, egg, and vanilla. While whisking, stream in melted butter and whisk to combine.

Step 2:


Gently fold dry ingredients into milk mixture until just combined.(Do not overmix)

Step 3:


Preheat a pan over medium heat until hot but not scorching, 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 4:


Melt enough butter to cover surface of pan, then pour about 1/4 cup batter onto the pan. Cook pancake until bubbles form on top and sides start to lift from pan, 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 5:


Flip and cook until bottom is golden brown and pancake is fluffy, 2 to 3 minutes.
