Cucumber Salad

15 Mins
X

Ingredients

10 Persian cucumbers, diced
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 cup chickpeas
¼ finely sliced red onion
½ cup crumbled feta cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
¼ cup olive oil
Sea salt, to taste

Directions

Step 1:


Begin by gathering and prepping all of your ingredients according to the ingredient list above to have ready and organized for use.

Step 2:

Place the diced cucumbers, the cherry tomatoes, the chickpeas, the red onion, ¼ cup of the feta cheese, half of the dill, half of the parsley and half of the mint into a large bowl.

Step 3:

Add the lemon juice and the olive oil, along with a couple of good pinches of sea salt, and gently toss together to combine.

Step 4:

Spoon the salad out onto a serving platter or into a salad bowl, and sprinkle over top the remaining ¼ cup of feta cheese and the remaining herbs, and serve.
