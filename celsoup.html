Celery Soup

20 Mins
X

Ingredients

3 tablespoons butter
8 large celery ribs, thinly sliced
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
3 garlic cloves
2 medium yellow potatoes, peeled and small diced
4 cups vegetable broth
¾ teaspoon kosher salt
¼ teaspoon dried dill
½ cup Italian parsley leaves
2 tablespoons heavy cream


Directions

Step 1:


In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion, celery and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 to 6 minutes until the onions are transparent.

Step 2:

Add the chopped potato, vegetable broth, and kosher salt and bring to a simmer. Simmer for 12 to 15 minutes until the potatoes are tender.

Step 3:

Carefully transfer the hot soup to a blender using a ladle. Add the dill and parsley and blend until creamy and pureed.

Step 4:

Add the heavy cream and pulse to combine. Serve warm, garnished with celery leaves, more chopped parsley, and a drizzle of cream.
