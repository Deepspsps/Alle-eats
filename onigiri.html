Onigiri

Ingredients

1 to 2 sheets dried nori seaweed
4 cups steamed Japanese rice
Kosher salt, to taste
1 ounce black sesame seeds







Directions

Step 1:


Cut each nori sheet (if using) into 8 or 9 strips and put about a 1/2 cup of steamed rice in a rice bowl.

Step 2:

Rub some salt on your wet hands. Place steamed rice in your hand and form into a triangle, making sure it is dense and thick.

Step 3:

Put your favorite filling, such as umeboshi or grilled salmon, on rice and push the filling into rice lightly.

Step 4:

Hold rice between palms. Form rice into a round, a triangle, or a cylinder by pressing lightly with both palms, securing filling in the middle. Roll rice ball in your hands a few times, pressing lightly.

Step 5:

Wrap rice ball with a strip or two of nori (if using), or sprinkle some sesame seeds on them (if using).
