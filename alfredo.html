Alfredo Pasta

45 mins

Ingredients

1 teaspoon garlic minced
¼ cup sweet corn
¼ cup bell pepper, broccoli
For the White Sauce
1.5 tablespoons butter
1.5 tablespoons flour
1.5 cups milk
2-3 tablespoons cheese grated
2 tablespoons heavy cream (optional)
¼ teaspoon red chili flakes and oregano
black pepper and salt (as per taste)

Directions


Step 1:

In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Then stir in the salt.


Step 2:

Add the dry pasta to the water, and stir a few times to prevent the pasta from sticking together.


Step 3:

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions (about 10-12 minutes), occasionally stirring, until softer according to your desired texture.


Step 4:

Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some starchy water for the sauce and drain the pasta. Set the cooked pasta aside.


Step 5:

Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant for about 30 seconds.


Step 6:

Then add mixed veggies like carrot, bell pepper, broccoli, sweet corn, and salt. Cook the veggies till they are tender but still crunchy, for around 2 minutes. Turn off the flame and keep it aside.


Step 7:

Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add all-purpose flour, and stir continuously until well combined and smooth. Cook for a minute until the raw smell of the flour goes away. Do not over-roast the flour, and ensure the color of the flour doesn’t change.


Step 8:

Slowly add in milk and stir continuously with a whisk until no lumps are left. Then add the remaining milk and whisk to combine. Cook it for about 4-5 minutes stirring constantly, until the sauce boils and thickens and then add all the sauted vegetables and give it a good mix before serving.


Ingredients

1 pack of refrigerated cheese
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
3 tablespoons butter
½ pound sliced fresh mushrooms
1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
2 cups heavy whipping cream
⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon salt

Directions

Step 1:

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add Cheese. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tortellini float to the top and the filling is hot, about 5 minutes. Drain.


Step 2:

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken breast in hot oil until no longer pink in the center, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.


Step 3:

Melt butter in another skillet over medium heat; cook and stir mushrooms and garlic powder until mushrooms begin to soften, about 3 minutes. Stir heavy cream into mushrooms and bring to a boil.


Step 4:

Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly thickened, about 3 minutes. Add Parmesan cheese, parsley, and salt. Continue to cook until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.


Step 5:

Stir Cheese and chicken into mushroom-cream sauce. Simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes.
