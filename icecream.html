Ice cream

4 hours
X

Nutrition Label

425 Calories per portion
Total Fat 25g 32%
Saturated Fat 15g 77%

Ingredients

1½ cups heavy cream
1½ cups sour milk
⅔ cup sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
⅛ teaspoon sea salt






Directions


Step 1:

In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, sour milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt.


Step 2:

Warm over medium-low heat, whisking often, for 5 minutes, or until the sugar dissolves and the mixture is warmed through.


Step 3:

Pour into a heatproof bowl, cover, and chill for 2 hours or overnight.
