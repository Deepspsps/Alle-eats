Ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil or avocado oil
1/2 red onion, chopped
8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
1 red bell pepper,
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons cumin and chlli powder
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
15 ounces black beans,
Juice of ½ lime
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Corn tortillas, for serving

Directions

Step 1:


In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium high heat. Add the onion, zucchini, mushrooms, and bell pepper.

Step 2:


Cook until vegetables are softened, stirring occasionally, about 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.

Step 3:


Add the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and oregano with 1 tablespoon of water. Stir until well combined.

Step 4:


Gently stir in the black beans. Add the lime juice and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Step 5:


To assemble the tacos, place the veggie filling in a warm corn tortilla. Top with desired toppings and serve immediately.

Ingredients

1 pound chicken breast halves(cut into bite size pieces)
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon lime juice
½ teaspoon garlic and onion powder
1 bay leaf
1 package corn tortillas
2 large tomatoes, chopped
1 head lettuce, shredded
1 package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 jar salsa
1 container sour cream

Directions

Step 1:


Combine chicken, olive oil, lime juice, and Worcestershire sauce in a large skillet over medium heat.

Step 2:


Stir in garlic powder, onion powder, and bay leaf. Simmer until chicken is tender and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 3:


Meanwhile, warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave until soft. When chicken is fully cooked, transfer to serving bowl.

Step 4:


Place chopped tomatoes, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream in serving dishes.
